Past,Present,Future, Composite Art, year

I feel like the picture I created didn't really erase any meaning to it but instead, more meaning to it. Instead of erasing items I kept overlapping them and adding more images to one picture. I feel like, by doing this it creates a bigger meaning of stating the true meaning behind it. This picture is an image of my front house. The gray picture on top on the image represents the past and how my " tree " looked like in 2011 and it brings back memories connected with it. The vibrant picture of my neighbor house present a bright moment in my life, it was in 2013 when I realized I was starting a new chapter in my life as a teenager. The pool painted bright blue is in 2015 as shown as a happy moment and best summer I had. I feel like this picture will be shown in a poster large, so people can see exactly what parts are old and new and what parts are changed.
